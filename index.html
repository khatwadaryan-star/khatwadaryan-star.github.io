Aryan Khatwad's Favorite Movies

1. Interstellar

  • This film is set in a future where Earth is suffering from a catastrophic blight, prompting a group of astronauts to travel through space to find a new home for humanity.
  • The emotional core of the relationship between the protagonist, Cooper, and his daughter, Murph makes this film emotionally powerful.
  • Additionally, the visual spectacle and soundtrack add to the immersive experience of this film.
